OUR STORY

Clean energy. Green energy.

Solar power is energy from the sun, converted into thermal or electrical energy — the cleanest and most abundant renewable energy source available, and India has some of the richest solar resources in the world.

Modern technology can harness this energy to generate electricity, provide light and heat water for domestic, commercial and industrial use. Saritam Power brings it all under one roof — and makes going solar an effortless experience.
